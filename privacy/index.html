Privacy Policy

Your privacy, fully protected.

Ew AI was built with a simple principle: what happens on your phone stays on your phone. We don't collect, store, or sell your data. Period.

Overview

The short version

Ew AI does not collect, store, or share any personal information about you. We have no user accounts, no analytics, no ad tracking, and no servers storing your data. The app sends your food photos to OpenAI's API to power the analysis — that's the only time any data leaves your device, and we never see it ourselves.

Data Collection

What we don't collect

We want to be completely explicit. Ew AI collects none of the following:

Name or contact information
Email address
Location data or GPS
Device identifiers
Usage analytics or events
Crash reports

Browsing or search history
Health or fitness data
Purchase history

Any other personal data

There are no user accounts to create, no sign-in required, and no profile of you is ever built.

App Permissions

Camera & Photo Library

Ew AI requests access to your camera and photo library for one reason only: so you can take or select a photo of food to analyze. These permissions are never used for anything else.

  • Photos you take or select are loaded directly into the app on your device.
  • Your photos are never saved to our servers, and we never store copies of them.
  • Access is only used when you actively initiate a scan — the app does not access your camera or library in the background.
  • You can revoke these permissions at any time in iOS Settings without affecting anything other than the app's ability to scan food.
How Images Are Handled

What happens to your food photo

Here is exactly what happens to your image when you tap "Disgust Me":

1

Resized on your device

Your photo is resized and compressed locally on your iPhone or iPad (max 1024px, JPEG compression) before anything leaves your device. This reduces file size and speeds up the analysis.

2

Sent to OpenAI for analysis

The compressed image is sent to OpenAI's API, which identifies the food, estimates its calories, and crafts the transformation description and disgusting image. This is the only moment your image leaves your device.

3

Displayed in the app

The AI-generated result is downloaded and shown to you in the app. The original and transformed images exist only in the app's memory during your session.

4

Discarded when you're done

When you tap "Try Another" or close the app, all images are cleared from memory. Nothing is persisted to your device's storage or to any server we control.

Third-Party Services

OpenAI

Ew AI uses OpenAI's API to power food analysis (GPT-4o) and image transformation (gpt-image-1). When you submit a food photo, it is transmitted to OpenAI's servers for processing.

We do not control OpenAI's data practices. OpenAI may retain API inputs for a limited period (typically up to 30 days) in accordance with their own policies. We encourage you to review OpenAI's Privacy Policy for full details on how they handle data submitted through their API.

Aside from OpenAI, Ew AI does not integrate with any other third-party analytics, advertising, or data-sharing services.

Children's Privacy

Not for children under 13

Ew AI is not directed at children under the age of 13. The app's content — which includes graphic and deliberately revolting AI-generated imagery — is intended for a general adult audience. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has used the app, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps.
