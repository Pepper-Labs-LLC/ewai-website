Privacy Policy

Your privacy, fully protected.

Ew AI was built with a simple principle: what happens on your phone stays on your phone. We don't collect, store, or sell your data.

Overview

The short version

For those who don't want to read the full policy — here's what matters most.

Ew AI does not collect, store, or share any personal information about you. There are no user accounts, no analytics, no ad tracking, and no servers of ours that store your data. When you scan food, your photo is sent to OpenAI's API to power the analysis — that's the only time any data leaves your device, and we never see it ourselves.

App Permissions

Camera & Photo Library

Ew AI requests access to your camera and photo library for one reason only: so you can photograph or select a food image to analyze. These permissions are not used for anything else.

  • Photos you take or select are loaded directly into the app on your device — they go nowhere until you tap "Disgust Me."
  • Your photos are never saved to our servers. We don't store any copies.
  • Access is only activated when you deliberately initiate a scan. The app never accesses your camera or photo library in the background.
  • You can revoke camera or photo access at any time in iOS Settings without affecting anything other than the app's ability to scan food.
Image Handling

What happens to your food photo

Here's the exact lifecycle of your image from the moment you tap "Disgust Me."

1

Resized on your device

Before anything leaves your device, your photo is resized and compressed locally on your iPhone or iPad — maximum 1024px on the long edge, JPEG quality 0.8. This reduces file size and speeds up the analysis.

2

Sent to OpenAI for analysis

The compressed image is transmitted to OpenAI's API (GPT-4o for food identification and gpt-image-1 for image transformation). This is the only point at which your image leaves your device — and it goes directly to OpenAI, not to us.

3

Result displayed in the app

The AI-generated transformation is downloaded and shown to you on the results screen. Both the original and the generated image exist only in the app's active memory during your session.

4

Cleared when you're done

When you tap "Try Another" or close the app, all images are cleared from memory. Nothing is written to your device's permanent storage, and nothing is retained on any server we control.

Third-Party Services

OpenAI

Ew AI uses OpenAI's API to power food analysis (GPT-4o) and image transformation (gpt-image-1). When you submit a food photo, it is transmitted to OpenAI's servers for processing. We do not control OpenAI's data practices.

OpenAI may retain API inputs for a limited period in accordance with their own policies. We encourage you to review OpenAI's Privacy Policy for full details on how they handle data submitted through their API.

Other than OpenAI, Ew AI does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, crash-reporting, or data-sharing services.

Children's Privacy

Not for children under 13

Ew AI is not directed at children under the age of 13. The app's content — including graphic, deliberately revolting AI-generated imagery and dark humor — is intended for a general adult audience.

We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has used the app and has a concern, please contact us at the address below and we will respond promptly.
